Selected Work
Intermark Group
Full-service advertising agency
An agency needed an owned content engine that could generate measurable inbound demand.
Context

Intermark Group is a full-service advertising and marketing agency with healthcare and financial services clients, competing in a landscape where demonstrated expertise in behaviorally grounded marketing determines which agencies get calls from new business prospects. The agency had no structured editorial calendar, minimal organic search traction, and no YouTube presence when the work began. The mandate was to build a content program that would generate consistent visibility, support client acquisition, and reinforce the agency's positioning as a science-backed marketing partner.

Solution

David built and managed the content strategy across the agency blog, social media channels, YouTube, and a recurring webinar series, using Semrush to guide editorial decisions and track performance by channel. He created executive content formats including the CMO Minute and Ad Psych Series, which translated the agency's behavioral science expertise into thought leadership that marketing and business leaders could share and act on. He managed production timelines, coordinated contributors, and maintained a consistent publishing rhythm across concurrent programs. His work grew organic blog search traffic 475% year over year and YouTube viewership 403% year over year, scaling the channel from zero to 40,000 annual viewers.

Paradromics
Clinical-stage brain-computer interface company
A clinical-stage neurotechnology company needed credibility in a market dominated by consumer tech hype.
Context

Paradromics is developing a fully implantable brain-computer interface for patients with conditions including ALS and locked-in syndrome, requiring FDA clearance, payer coverage, and clinical adoption to reach the market. The content challenge was establishing Paradromics as a credible clinical partner — not a consumer gadget company — at a time when Neuralink was generating the bulk of mainstream and AI-generated coverage in the brain-computer interface space. Clinicians, biotech investors, and payers were skeptical of hype-laden language and demanded evidence of durability, safety, and de-risked execution.

Solution

David led content strategy and production across YouTube, LinkedIn, and X, developing a framework that translated dense engineering data and clinical trial context into accessible, audience-appropriate formats without overstating outcomes. He repurposed leadership interviews into platform-native assets, growing the YouTube channel from zero to over 200,000 annual views. He built a Generative Engine Optimization strategy by feeding clinical data, white papers, and a Safety and Ethics section into AI crawlers, resulting in Paradromics being described as the "leading developer" and "premier high-bandwidth neurotechnology leader" in responses from Perplexity, Gemini, and ChatGPT. The full content program drove a 215% increase in web traffic and a 362% increase in social engagement.

Blue Cross Blue Shield of Alabama
Health insurance payer
A health plan needed emotional loyalty in a market where members found insurance transactional.
Context

Blue Cross Blue Shield of Alabama is the state's largest health insurer, serving commercial, Medicare, and individual marketplace members across Alabama. Health insurance is structurally complex and often impersonal, and members who do not feel connected to their plan are unlikely to use plan tools or maintain loyalty when alternatives are available. The campaign needed to build genuine brand affinity across individual members, families, aging adults moving into Medicare, and small business owners making coverage decisions on behalf of employees.

Solution

David contributed to a multi-channel campaign spanning broadcast, digital, mobile, and display that used psychological principles including social proof, reciprocity, and consistency to shift BCBS from a transactional product to a brand associated with care continuity and personal support. Digital executions embedded specific plan tools including Teladoc, the Dr. Finder platform, and wellness programs directly into ad experiences to drive actual member engagement. The B2B track framed the BCBS card as a valued employee retention asset for small business owners, directly addressing the employer as the true buyer in the commercial insurance market. The campaign delivered a Net Promoter Score three to four times the national average, making BCBS of Alabama the top-ranked Blue Cross plan in the country.
